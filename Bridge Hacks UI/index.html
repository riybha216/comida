Behind Comida

Making food from leftovers.

As the amount of leftovers and meals made at home increase due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the amount of food going to the landfill is increasing as well, allowing a drastic increase in food wastage all over the world. Food wastage has a significant impact on our environment, and allows methane, a greenhouse gas, to develop. The development of methane contributes largely to global warming, allowing our planet's environment to deteriorate.

In order to stop this from occurring, I created Comida, which is a platform that generates well-liked recommendations on what to create with your leftovers. People can also see warnings if the food is deemed to be expired, as well as tips to better preserve the food in the future. Also, if the food can be turned into fertilizer or be disposed of correctly, these items will appear as well.
